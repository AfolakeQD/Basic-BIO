Let's get to meet Imam Tirmidhi briefly

Full Name:- Muhammad bn Isa bn Sawrah al-Tirmidhi
Father's Name:- Isa bn Sawrah
Birth Date:- 209AH (9 October 824)
Place of Birth:- Tirmidhi (Called Termez in Persian, located in Uzbekistan). He is said to be Persian.
Died:- 279AH(892CE) in Termez Uzbekistan.
Some of his work:-
  • Jami at Tirmidhi
  • Shamail
  • Muhammadiyah
