Let's get to meet Imam Muslim briefly

Full Name:- Abu Husayn Asaakir ad-Din Muslim bn al-Hajjaj bn Muslim bn Ward bn Kawshadh al-Qushayri an Naysaburi
Father's Name:- Al-Hajjaj bn Muslim
Birth Date:- Different dates were reported, some of which are;
  1. 202AH(817/818)
  2. 204AH(819/820)
  3. 206AH(821/822)
Place of Birth:- Nashapur(Iran)
Died:- 25 Rajab 261AH(May 875) in Nasarabad(Iran) at age 55
His notable work:- Sahih Muslim

Based on the year of death and his age at the time of death, the likely correct year of his birth is 206AH but Allah knows best.
