Let's get to meet Imam Al-Bukhari briefly

Full Name:- Muhammad Ibn Isma'eel Ibn Ibrahim Ibn al-Mughirah Ibn Bardizbah al-Ju'fi al-Bukhari
Father's Name:- Ismail bn Ibrahim
Birth Date:- I3 Shawwal I94AH (2I July 8I0)
Place of Birth:- Bukhara (located in Uzbekistan). He is said to be Persian.
Died:- 1 Shawwal 256AH (1 September 870CE) at 60 years of age, at Khartank Samarkand, Uzbekistan.
Some of his work:-
  • Sahih Al-Bukhari
  • Al Adab wal Mufrad
