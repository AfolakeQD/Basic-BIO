Let's get to meet Imam Ahmad briefly

Full Name:- Ahmad bn Hanbal bn Hilal bn Asad bn Idris bn AbdAllah bn Hayyan
Father's Name:- Muhammad bn Hanbal
Birth Date:- Rabiul Awwal 164AH (November 780)
Place of Birth:- Baghdad(in Iraq).
Died:- Rabiul Awwal 241AH(2 August 855CE) at age 74 .
Some of his work:-
  • Al Musnad
  • Fadail Sahabah
  • Risalah fi al Salah li Ahli l Qiblah
